Features

  • Comprehensive access to major blockchain networks

    Connect to major networks and protocols. Build on scalable and reliable infrastructure from Infura with 99.9% uptime.

  • Guarantee 100% uptime with failover support

    If Infura API endpoints ever become unavailable, your dapps stay connected through our partners on the Decentralized Infrastructure Network (DIN)

  • Ship faster with APIs that power MetaMask

    Enhance your dapps’ capabilities with our suite of APIs behind the MetaMask wallet, including Gas API and Trace API.
